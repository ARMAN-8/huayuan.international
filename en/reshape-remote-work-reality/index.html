Reshape Your Remote Work Reality

With Lenovo Devices

The transition to remote work is now an established fact. But it brought its share of challenges, for managers as well as for their employees.

Indeed, we’ve never had more ways or more places in which to work, but today’s work environment is stretching our ability to concentrate, multitask and manage our time. Distractions are multiplying, the boundary between work and personal life is vanishing, and your top talents might feel left to themselves.

So how can you offer your teams an optimized work experience in this context? How can you make sure your teams have everything they need to deliver?

Designed for the New Reality

It is crucial to thoroughly understand and consider the needs of your employees who work remotely. It’s more than just a chair, a desk and a VPN access. They need high-performance devices designed for this new reality they face on a daily basis. These devices end up playing a crucial role in enhancing their employee experience and fostering collaboration between teams, even when they’re working remotely.

Designed for business, optimized for mobility, the Lenovo ThinkPad series help you leverage remote work to become more competitive, increase productivity, and attract top talents.

Whether you need increased security and biometric authentication, powerful processors for heavy workloads, or robust equipment for extreme work environments, Lenovo’s ThinkPad series are made to meet your expectations.

Empower your employees with productivity boosting devices designed to enhance collaboration and employee experience.

Performance, Functionality & Security
ThinkPad X1 Titanium Yoga

The new ThinkPad X1 Titanium Yoga powered by the Intel® Evo™ vPro® platform delivers what IT needs and users want. This ultrathin, ultralight 2-in-1 boasts an impressive titanium design and delivers both tablet and notebook functionality that takes you anywhere.

High-powered portable productivity, just 11.5 mm thin, 13.5” 2K touch display and full ThinkPad keyboard, laptop or tablet mode.

ThinkPad X1 Fold

The ThinkPad X1 Fold provides a split-screen experience. Fold it like a book for two side-by-side displays—perfect for when you want to compare something, take notes, read, or just multitask. Turn it so the split screen appears top to bottom, and you can sketch, jot notes, or use the on-screen keyboard to type.

With a brilliant and seamless folding OLED screen, the ThinkPad X1 Fold provides a full 13 inch 2K display.

ThinkPad X1 Nano

The X1 Nano is the first ThinkPad laptop to use the Intel® Evo™ platform, delivering a powerhouse combination of performance, responsiveness, battery life, and stunning visuals.

With up to 11th Gen Intel® Core™ vPro® i7 processors, you can count on an exceptional experience, anywhere.

ThinkPad X13

The ThinkPad X13 proves you don’t have to sacrifice performance for portability. Though small in size, this professional notebook packs a big punch thanks to significant enhancements in processors, memory, and wireless connectivity, all while boasting much better battery life.

ThinkPad T Series

Built to perform and engineered to endure, ThinkPad T Series laptops have you covered in the office, out in the field, and everywhere in between. Productivity-rich features like epic battery life, blazing-fast USB –C Thunderbolt® ports, and powerful processors to keep you up and running.

ThinkPad L Series

If it’s productivity and price that matter most, ThinkPad L Series deliver. These powerful laptops and 2-in-1s boast legendary reliability, spill-resistant keyboards, security options, and superior web-conferencing—all at cost-conscious pricing.

ThinkSmart Bar & ThinkSmart Cam

The new ThinkSmart Bar transforms collaboration rooms into more productive spaces where ideas can be fully expressed and explored, while the new ThinkSmart Cam creates immersive, productive spaces where everyone can be fully engaged and collaborative regardless of where they are located.

Built for Remote Work

  • Unparalleled Mobility
    The portability and versatility of ThinkPad PCs make you more mobile and agile in a fast-changing world.
  • Microsoft 365 Compatibility
    Thinkpad devices are designed to get the best of Microsoft 365 tools like Outlook, increasing productivity with software you are familiar with and facilitating collaboration thanks to the powerful Teams platform.
  • Designed with Security in Mind

  • Optimized Efficiency
    Benefit from enhanced user experience, ergonomics, and usability helping you getting things done for your organization.

More accessible than ever with DaaS

Your organization can acquire all the Lenovo devices it needs to empower your teams without any prohibitive upfront cost. Thanks to the DaaS (Device as a Service) subscription model, your devices are delivered and used as a service, not as depreciating assets.

In other words, there are no initial expenses; only a monthly fee. Your business capital will not be used to acquire IT equipment, and will not have to compromise on the quality and performance of the devices you need.

ITI can help your organization leverage the DaaS model. You will have the devices and tools you need, all bundled up in a comprehensive and accessible plan that optimizes your cash flow.
